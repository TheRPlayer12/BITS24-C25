    ACbio – Quiz 2024

    ACBio is organizing the Inter-University Biology Quiz Competition for the undergraduates of State and UGC approved Universities in Sri Lanka for the 7th consecutive year in 2024. The competition aims at providing undergraduates with a novel educational experience and enhancing their interest in Biology. The competition will also provide an ideal opportunity for collaboration and inspiration among students, academics and universities across the country.

    Inter-University Biology Challenge Competition 2023

    ACBio is organizing an Annual Biology Challenge competition for interdisciplinary teams of undergraduates selected from state and none state universities in Sri Lanka to develop an attractive and challenging proposal to nourish the IOBSL theme “Sustainable Bio-economy” and to enhance the multi disciplinary interaction among the biology and non-biology students. Each team is expected to collaborate with an industrial partner or the Industry Liaison Centres of the University.
